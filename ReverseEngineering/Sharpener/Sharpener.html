DESIGN EVALUATION
Modern electric sharpeners essentially operate in the same way as the Gilbert sharpener presented above. The largest difference is having a more compact motor and plastic rather than glass casing.


Close up of gear reduction intended to output lower speed and higher torque. The Blades spin around a motionless pencil tip.


To allow the user to keep the sharpener plugged in continuously, there are two switches. The first is to only close the circuit when a pencil is inserted. The second is to close the circuit when the tray to collect shavings is fully seated in the sharpener casing.
